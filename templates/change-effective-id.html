Changing or correcting your identity data

{{pagetitle}} obtains identity data from Globus. Globus obtains identity data from the organizations you've logged in with or have linked in Globus. These organizations are called identity providers.

When you visited {{pagetitle}}, your effective identity provider was {{primaryidp}}. Globus will always use the same effective identity provider for this app, but other apps may request a different effective identity provider.

The data shown in your ID Token, from token introspection, and from the Identities API mostly comes from this identity provider. To change or correct the data returned by these interfaces, go to the effective identity provider's website and use its user profile feature to make changes. The next time you authenticate using that identity provider in any app that uses Globus, the data in your ID Token and shown by the Identities API and token introspection will be updated with the new information.

XSEDE apps request XSEDE as the effective identity provider. When using these apps, your name, email address, username, and organization come from your XSEDE user profile. (View your profile in the XSEDE user portal to compare them.)
